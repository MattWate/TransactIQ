About TransactIQ

Independent payments advisory for organisations that need strategy to move.

TransactIQ brings specialist payments knowledge, data-led thinking and execution discipline to consumer and commercial payment challenges.

Who we are

A specialist consulting partner for payments performance.

TransactIQ exists for organisations that need payments expertise to translate into commercial outcomes. The firm works across consumer payment portfolios, commercial payment solutions, analytics, AI-enabled insight and implementation support.

The approach is deliberately focused. TransactIQ is not positioned as a broad, generic consultancy. It is a specialist advisory partner for payment businesses, financial institutions, fintechs and platforms that need clearer decisions and better execution.

Operating belief
Payments consulting should make decisions clearer, performance more visible and execution more achievable.
Our team

Led by founders with deep payments expertise.

TransactIQ is led by Harold Van Eeden and Charles Campbell, with a scalable advisory model designed to bring in specialist payments, risk, data and implementation expertise as client engagements require it.

H

Harold Van Eeden

Managing Partner | Strategist and expert in commercial payments

Harold Van Eeden is a seasoned payments industry executive with over 25 years of experience driving growth, innovation and transformation across global and emerging markets.

Prior to TransactIQ, Harold held senior leadership roles at Visa, including Senior Director, CEMEA B2B Solutions and Director, Sub-Saharan Africa Commercial Business. In these roles, he led strategic initiatives to expand commercial and B2B payment capabilities, strengthen partnerships and accelerate the adoption of modern payment solutions across diverse markets.

Harold is recognised for his ability to translate complex challenges into scalable, high-impact solutions.

C

Charles Campbell

Managing Partner | Payments, consulting and analytics professional

With over 20 years of global experience across advisory, implementation services and training, Charles Campbell has built and led high-performing teams that help financial institutions turn data and strategy into tangible commercial results.

His career spans senior roles at Visa, where he headed the CEMEA Consulting & Analytics implementation services practice, as well as specialist analytics firms Qarar and Principa, where he delivered credit risk analytics and consulting engagements for banks and lenders worldwide.

Charles holds advanced degrees in Operations Research from Stellenbosch University and brings a rare combination of quantitative depth and commercial acumen to every engagement.
